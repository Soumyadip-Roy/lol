Mission & Objectives

The main purpose of the this Association is to provide an organization through which the Alumni of AIT around the world, and faculty, staff and students of the institute can interact with each other for mutual benefit and to further the objectives outlined in the Mission Statement.

Alumni :To facilitate improvement in networking among alumni by providing a platform for social interactions and enable higher levels of interactions and enable interactions between alumni for mutual benefit in academic and/or business areas.

Institute :To facilitate and encourage alumni to contribute towards improvement in the status of the institute in areas pertaining to academics, infrastructure, industry interactions and any other area that the alumni and the institute feel appropriate.

T & P Committee

Objectives

  • To make each final year candidate employable so that he/she can start a bright career in IT, ITeS, Consulting, Manufacturing, Engineering, Fintech and other industries, by conducting effective training modules.
  • To provide excellent placement opportunities to most of the students by inviting leading industries from all domains for campus recruitment.
  • To establish a good liaison with maximum industries who have academic connect for GET hiring and Internships.
  • To sustain and enhance alumni interaction in best possible ways like alumni meets and other interactive activities.
