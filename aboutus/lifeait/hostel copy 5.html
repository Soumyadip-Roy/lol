  • Hostel
    • AIT provides hostel accommodation for boys and girls students pursuing Undergraduate and Postgraduate studies in AIT Campus. Hostels are as under:-
    • Boys Hostels - Capacity 1130
      • Abdul Kalam Hostel
      • Vishwesvariya Hostel
      • Homi Bhabha Hostel
      • SN Bose Hostel
      • Sarabhai Hostel
      • Ramanujan Hostel
      • Capt Gurbir Singh Hostel
    • Girls Hostel - Capacity 175
      • Kalpana Chawla Hostel
  • Facilities
    • AIT offers hostel rooms with following facilities:-
    • Each room is provided with tube light, fan, charging points and furniture including table, chair, cupboard, book shelf, bed etc for each students separately.
    • Wi-Fi with very good speed
    • RO purified water for drinking is available on all floors
    • Solar water heater supported by electrical water heater
    • Tiled bathrooms with modern amenities
    • Study hall on roof top
    • 24/7 power back up
    • Recreational Room with TV, Pool Table, Foos ball Table, Carom board etc.
    • Well-equipped central gymnasium for boys and dedicated gymnasium for girls
    • 24/7 ambulance facility, Nursing Assistant and one visiting Doctor
  • Discipline
    • Ragging is strictly prohibited
    • Smoking/ consumption of alcohol/ drugs is prohibited
    • Hostel wardens as well as Anti Ragging Squad are deputed to monitor the situation inside the campus and hostels
