Mess

The Students' Mess Committee at AIT takes charge of the menu, ensuring a diverse selection of healthy meals at affordable prices. The kitchen and dining hall are equipped with state-of-the-art gadgets and appliances to support efficient food preparation and service. In addition to offering a variety of dishes, the mess also prioritizes the health and well-being of students by conducting regular medical check-ups for the food handlers, ensuring the overall safety and quality of the meals provided.
