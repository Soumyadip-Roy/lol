RO Water Plant

AIT's water purification system boasts a capacity of 1000 liters per hour, featuring cutting-edge technology such as an FRP Reverse Osmosis Plant with a stainless steel stand, UV system, a 1000-liter storage tank, and a transfer pump. The pipeline connections extend to key areas on the campus, including hostels, the library, academic blocks, and staff quarters, ensuring a seamless supply of purified water throughout the institute.
