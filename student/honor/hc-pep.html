Penalties /Punishments


The Honour Council may recommend any of the following punishments, singly or with other punishments, to be awarded to any student indulging in any act which violates the Honour Code, depending on the severity of the act/misconduct:

Verbal warning/ censure/ reprimand

Written warning/ censure/ reprimand

Fine (Amount to be specified)

Withholding of results (Period to be specified)

Rustication (Period to be specified)

Expulsion (Period to be specified)

Students staying in AIT hostel may be punished with expulsion from hostel, for an act he/she committed
