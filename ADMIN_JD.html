Overview

The administration of a college plays a crucial role in its functioning. It typically includes various departments such as academic affairs, student services, finance, and human resources. Administrators oversee policies, manage resources, and ensure the smooth operation of the institution. They collaborate with faculty and staff to create a positive learning environment, address student needs, and uphold the college's mission. Joint Director’s Corner

Col MK Prasad (Retd)

BE (Civil), ME (Str), PGDPM & HR, DBA (IIMA), DBC(CDAC) Jt Director, AIT

Email :jd@aitpune.edu.in

Col Manoj Kumar Prasad (Retd), an Engineering Officer served in Indian Army from Jun 1986 to May 2018. He superannuated from CME Pune after 32 years of experience in various combat and works appointments.

He is an alumni of BIT Sindri, Gold Medalist of IISc Bangalore, Post Graduate Diploma in PM&HR from SIMS Pune, Diploma in Business Computing from CDAC besides alumnus of IIM Ahmedabad.

He served 202 Engineer Regiment five tenures, in MES as SO2 (Design), CWE (Jabalpur), CWE (Vishakhapatnam), Additional Chief Engineer (Works) New Delhi, two tenures as Instructor at CME Pune and one tenure as instructor at MEG&C Bangalore. He served as University Engineer, Nalanda University and Col GS (Engrs), Strategic Force Command. He has privilege to serve Army/ Navy/ Air Force/ Strategic Force Command/ Nalanda University.

After retirement from defence forces, he served as Project Head (Civil) at Sterlite Technologies Ltd (Vedanta Group) at Gurgaon and constructed 34 data centers PAN India for Indian Navy under project Varun.

Message

Army Institute of Technology (AIT), an engineering college run under the aegis of Army Welfare Education Society (AWES). The college offers four undergraduate courses and one postgraduate course. The college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and affiliated to the Savitribai Phule Pune University. The college is built in a sprawling campus of 30 acres, with a blend of beautiful green landscaping and aesthetical elegance. Excellent education is the foundation for a secure, prosperous and exciting future. Our students, the learners of the day, are well trained to face the challenges of highly competitive future and trained well to bridge the gap between industries and educational institutes by a well-crafted internship programs. The college is committed to provide continuous support and encouragement for the students, having innovative ideas for initiating start-up companies as part of “Make in India” Mission. Our students are now started winning various awards and prize moneys on regular basis.

The institute has grown over the years in pursuit of their vision and crossed several milestones of excellence. This has become possible only due to continued patronage of the magnanimous management, tireless efforts of experienced, qualified faculties and cooperative adm staff members and worthy contributions of its dedicated students and alumni. AIT has a consistent track record of above 90% placements for past seven years.

I wish all the students a very happy and academically highly rewarding student life at AIT and hope that you all will enhance the pride and prestige of this esteemed institution. 'Onward to Glory', 'Jai Hind'.
